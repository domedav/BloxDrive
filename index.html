☁️ BloxDrive
Turn your unlimited Roblox inventory into a secure, infinite, high-speed virtual hard drive. Banish storage worries forever.

What is BloxDrive?

Instead of paying monthly subscription fees for cloud storage, BloxDrive lets you store unlimited files completely for free. It splits your files, encrypts them securely on your computer, hides them inside Roblox decal images using steganography, and uploads them to Roblox servers.

To you, it looks just like an ordinary folder on your computer! You can drag, drop, open, and edit files, and they will load directly over the internet.

PC Requirements
  • Linux Operating System (FUSE kernel driver support is native to Linux)
  • Python 3.10 or higher installed
  • MySQL, MariaDB (or a free cloud TiDB database to sync between multiple computers)
  • FUSE package installed (sudo apt install fuse on Ubuntu/Debian)
Why Use It?
  • 100% Free: No subscriptions, no storage caps.
  • Completely Private: Files are encrypted with AES-256 before leaving your computer. Nobody (not even Roblox) can see what you store.
  • RAID-5 Protection: Link multiple accounts. If one gets banned, your files are automatically restored without losing a byte.
  • Bypass Limits: Clever chunking prevents Roblox from auto-resizing your data files.

Quick Launch Guide

Ready to start? Bypassing tech jargon, here is exactly how you launch your virtual drive:

1

Install dependencies

Open your terminal and run the quick installer:

bash quickinstall.sh
2

Configure your Database

Fill out database fields in settings.json, then initialize the database structure:

python3 src/main.py db init
3

Mount and use your storage

Run the drive mount script. It will ask for your Roblox credentials on first start:

./bloxdrive.sh start

You can now access your infinite drive at /tmp/bloxdrive_mnt!
